Dosa Recipe


Famous South Indian food

A savory rice pan cake

Ingredients

  • rice
  • baking soda
  • lentil(optional)

Process

  1. soak rice and lentil for few hours
  2. grind it
  3. rest it for overnight for fermentation
  4. add baking soda,mix it
  5. pour the 1/4 cup scoop of batter in the pan and spread it,cook it for 5 min
  6. Dosa READY
