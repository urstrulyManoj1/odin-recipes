Biryani Recipe


Famous South Indian food

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of South Asia. It is made with spices, vegetables, rice, and usually some type of meat

Ingredients

  • basmati rice
  • Indian spices
  • chichen
  • onions,garlic paste
  • tomatoes,kesari,chillies

Process

  1. soak basmati rice for few minutes and boil it for 15 minutes
  2. marinate chicken with yoghurt,indian spices like mirchi salt turmeric etc.
  3. in the large bowl fry onions, add vegetables and add the marinated chichen
  4. after some time pour the boiled basmati rice into the large bowl
  5. close the large bowl with a plate having maida flour as gum,cook it for 30 min
  6. Chicken-Biryani READY
