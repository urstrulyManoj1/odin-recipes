Idly Recipe


Famous South Indian food

A savory rice cake

Ingredients

  • rice
  • baking soda
  • lentil(optional)

Process

  1. soak rice and lentil for few hours
  2. grind it
  3. rest it for overnight for fermentation
  4. add baking soda,mix it
  5. pour the batter in the idly cooker,steam it for 30 min
  6. IDLY READY
